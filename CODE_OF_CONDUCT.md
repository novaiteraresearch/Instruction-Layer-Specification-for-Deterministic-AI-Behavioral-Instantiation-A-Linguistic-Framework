# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all public interactions associated with this repository, including issues, discussions, and any other communication channels linked from this repository.

## Standards

All participants in discussions or issue threads related to this repository are expected to:

- Communicate in a professional, respectful, and constructive manner.
- Focus discussion on the research work, its citation metadata, and related technical topics.
- Refrain from harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or disruptive behavior of any kind.
- Respect the proprietary nature of the underlying research and the intellectual property rights of Nova Itera Research Group.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks directed at the author, organization, or other participants.
- Demands or pressure to disclose proprietary implementation details not included in this repository.
- Attempts to circumvent or challenge the intellectual property protections stated in the LICENSE.
- Submission of issues or discussions in bad faith, including attempts to extract non-public information.

## Enforcement

The maintainer (Ariel J. Furlow, Nova Itera Research Group) reserves the right to:

- Close or lock issues and discussions that violate these standards.
- Restrict access to repository interaction features for participants who repeatedly violate this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For concerns related to conduct in this repository, contact the maintainer via the contact information provided in the SECURITY.md file.

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted for a proprietary research documentation repository and is governed by the standards of professional academic and research community conduct.
